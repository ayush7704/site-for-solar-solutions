On-Grid Solar Power Plant

solar panel is a device that collects sunlight and turns it into usable electricity. Solar panels are usually made of solar cells (made from the element silicon), wiring, a metal frame, and a glass cover. A typical solar panel is about four feet wide, and six feet tall.

Off-Grid Solar Power Plant

Off-grid Systems work independently of the grid but have batteries which can store the solar power generated by the system.Standalone or Off-Grid Solar Systems are installed in situations where mains power is not available, or the client wishes to become completely independent of the grid. All power is generated by the solar panels; a battery bank stores excess power for use at night or when the sun is not shining.

Solar Water Pump

Solar water pumps offer a game-changing advantage: they transform sunlight into reliable energy, liberating us from conventional power constraints. By ensuring constant water supply for various needs, these pumps not only cut operational costs but also reduce our carbon footprint. Embracing this technology signifies a leap towards greener, more self-sufficient practices, amplifying efficiency and savings while honoring our planet.

Solar Street Light

Solar street lights are water-resistance and weatherproof and have a low glare and low insect attrition rate. The solar panels in these lights convert solar energy into electrical energy that gets stored in the inbuilt battery and is utilized for dusk-to-dawn lighting operations

Solar Home Lighting System

Solar Home Lighting System: Home lighting System is powered by solar energy using solar cells that convert solar energy (sunlight) directly to electricity DC. The electricity can be directly used by DC appliances or can be stored in batteries and used for the purpose of lighting whenever required.
